Signal Gap Mesh welcomes everyone!

All you have to do:

  1. Get one of the supported LoRa radios
  2. Flash & configure your radio
  3. Download our app on your iPhone or Android
  4. Connect to your radio via bluetooth
  5. Open the app and say hi!

Do you have a question? Reach out to us; we provide assistance.

And email us to join our Slack channel, become an official member and attend our in-person meetings in South Florida.

You can also follow us on GitHub, Twitter, Instagram, Threads and YouTube.
